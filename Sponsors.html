WHY SPONSOR US ?

Help us to help you. Active co-operation is the path to mutual progress. We partake not only in some of the best competitions in the world but also present our Aircraft in national level technical events such as Abhiyantriki and Maker Mela. These events and competitions are attended by industry giants and other people from various fields of engineering. This makes up a large amount of people and exposure to such an amount of crowd can help in forwarding your company. Moreover, if you sponsor us you will present in all the marketing media that we create, that includes highlight videos, instagram stories and posts, posters and information brochures where your company will be labelled as sponsor. Team Onyx India will make sure that your company is advertised and represented in a professional manner and provide the right amount of exposure to the public eyes. Please do check out the about us section for more detailed insight to our projects.
