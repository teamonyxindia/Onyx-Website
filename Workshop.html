Aeromodelling Workshops

Team Onyx India conducts workshops on a yearly basis which witness participation from colleges all over Mumbai. This Workshop is split into two days for convenience. The basics of aerodynamics, design, propulsion and manufacturing methods are discussed on the first day while the students get a hands-on experience of building an aircraft on the second. The positive reviews from the attendees motivates us to give us our best to inculcate various activities related to aeromodelling.

AERO DESIGN WORKSHOP 2019

“Once you have tasted flight, you will forever walk the earth with your eyes turned skyward, for there you have been, and there you will always long to return.”
~ Leonardo da Vinci


Walking on these lines, Team Onyx India, the Aero-design team of KJSCE, brings you an opportunity that will help you delve deeper into the world of aviation. An Aeromodelling workshop, where you will learn about the design, manufacturing and flight of an aircraft under the guidance of an Aviation specialist. You will also get a certificate that will be certified by a researcher at IIT-Bombay. Planes once done can be taken by the participants.
